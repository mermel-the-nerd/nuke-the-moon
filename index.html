It wasn't just our idea: Project A119

Project A119 was a top-secret U.S. Air Force plan in the late 1950s to detonate a nuclear bomb on the Moon. The goal was to demonstrate American military and technological superiority during the Cold War, especially in response to the Soviet Union’s advancements in space exploration. The explosion would have been visible from Earth, showcasing U.S. capabilities. Although the plan was ultimately abandoned, it was deemed to be technically feasible.
